Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This extension (Playwright Locator Assistant) does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Collection

The extension processes data locally in your browser. It accesses the content of the web pages you visit only to generate Playwright locators when you use the extension.

No information is sent to any external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

No data is used outside your browser session. The extension does not collect personal, financial, or other sensitive information.

Security

Since no data leaves your device, your privacy and security are maintained.
