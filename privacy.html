Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This extension (Playwright Locator Assistant) does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Collection

All processing happens locally within your browser. The extension accesses and analyzes the content of web pages only to generate Playwright locators when you use it.

No data is sent to any external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

The extension does not use any collected data outside of your browser session. No personal, financial, or sensitive information is gathered.

Security

Since no data leaves your device, your privacy and security are maintained at all times.
